# Sephora Product & Customer Insight Analysis

## Business Context

This project simulates working as a Business Analyst at Sephora. The goal is to use product and customer review data to understand how customers experience Sephora’s products and how that feedback can guide better business decisions.

## Project Goals

* Understand product performance using ratings and engagement
* Identify products with high attention but low satisfaction
* Explore patterns in positive vs negative reviews

## Questions I’m Exploring

* Which products and brands perform best?
* Which products receive many reviews but low ratings?
* What words or themes appear in 1-star vs 5-star reviews?

## Data

Dataset: Sephora Products and Skincare Reviews (Kaggle)
Contains product info, ratings, review text, and engagement metrics.

## Tools

* SQL
* Excel
* Python
* Power BI
* GitHub

## Process

1. Explore and understand the data
2. Clean and prepare the dataset
3. Analyze product and brand performance
4. Explore customer sentiment
5. Build dashboards and summaries
